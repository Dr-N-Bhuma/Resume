PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

A dynamic leader, bold decision maker and a passionate teacher with more than two decades of experience in teaching and guiding Post Graduate students with a path breaking tenure as a vibrant Principal. A perfectionist who believes in integral development and collective growth.

An ambitious and determined leader who sees education as a means to acquiring knowledge, developing skills and imbibing ethics and values.

A firm disciplinarian and a compassionate team player. DG Vaishnav College has awarded Beyond Call of Service award for industrious work.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS AS PRINCIPAL

For Institution

As a Principal, has been a ground breaking pioneer who created greater opportunities for the students to learn and explore. Given a Shape and has taken the institution to new horizon for the first time in 22 years of history of college..

New Courses

With the academic background and knowledge of the industry in the emerging field of study and to keep in par with changing scenario, Introduced courses like B.Sc Visual communication, B.Sc Psychology and Vocational courses like B.Sc ID &D. Shift II course has been started based on students need.

Certificate Courses

Introduced a wide range of Add on certificate courses apart from its regular degree programs which the student can pursue along side. These courses provide students the requisite knowledge that would form the foundation for a future career.

IQAC

Established the Internal Quality Assurance Cell for the first time. To monitor and improve the teaching and learning process. The cell also organises workshops, seminars, and conferences for teaching and non- teaching staff to sustain quality and performance.

For Students

Clubs

Constituted various clubs and committees to equip the students with knowledge and skills and groom them to be self-reliant, resourceful and socially responsible citizens.

Students Council

Instituted Student Council “Maha Dhuhitha” to give students an opportunity to develop leadership qualities by organizing and carrying out various college activities.

MahaUtsav

Encouraged students to organize Maha Utsav (Mega Intercollegiate Culturals) to bring out the unique talents of Students. In addition to academic excellence due importance is given to co-curricular and extra curricular activities in and outside the college.

Sports Activities

“Mahalshmi Trophy “ an Intercollegiate tournament has been initiated for the sports development

For Faculty

A person who strives to provide positive work culture for the faculty members in order to work effectively Arranged various FDP programmes for faculty members to improve their quality and to enhance their knowledge in the changing era of technology.

Faculty member are encouraged to pursue refresher/orientation courses and actively participate in research activities by presenting papers in seminars and conferences enabling them to update their knowledge and keep abreast of changing trends in their area of specialization.

Extended support to the faculty to pursue research in order to sustain the standards of excellence to serve the students better.

Beside their academic growth, created opportunities for faculty members to relax, share and exhibit their other talents by conducting competitions, various get together and celebrations in the college.

Lockdown Activities

Organized more than 90 virtual programmes including national and international webinars, Faculty Development Programs and student activities during the lockdown.
